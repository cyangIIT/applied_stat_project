4.3 Hypothesis test on coefficients of alpha value is setting as 0.05

#H0: Bi = 0, i =0,1,2,3,4 
#Ha: Bi != 0
#Reject Ha fs F0 > F(0.05,4,37)
F_critical = qf(p=.05, df1=4, df=37, lower.tail=FALSE)
